About Profiles

Allowed IPs determines what traffic goes through the VPN:

  • 0.0.0.0/0,::/0 - All traffic (full tunnel)
  • 10.0.0.0/8,192.168.0.0/16 - Only private networks
  • specific IP/32 - Only specific destinations

DNS specifies which DNS servers clients should use:

  • 1.1.1.1,1.0.0.1 - Cloudflare DNS
  • 8.8.8.8,8.8.4.4 - Google DNS
  • 10.0.0.1 - Server's DNS (if configured)
